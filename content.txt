Out of Hours Service
NHS 111
If you require medical assistance outside normal practice hours, please contact the NHS 111 service by calling 111. The service is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and all calls to the service are free. If you contact the service, you may be;
Given telephone advice
Asked to attend a local Out of hours centre for a consultation
Referred to a local Accident and Emergency department, or
Offered a home visit by a deputising Doctor
In a Emergency
In the event of a medical emergency, please call 999 immediately to contact the ambulance service.
